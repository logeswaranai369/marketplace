Nextcloud is a self-hosted productivity platform — your own private cloud for files, calendars, contacts, and collaboration.

What you get when you deploy this app:

- Secure file sync and share across all your devices, with a web interface and desktop/mobile clients.
- Built-in calendar, contacts, and tasks that work with standard CalDAV/CardDAV clients.
- Collaborative document editing, deck boards, and team folders.
- Persistent storage for your data, automatic HTTPS, and daily backups handled by the platform.

After deploy, open the app to finish the first-run admin setup, then invite your team or connect your devices. You can attach a custom domain and scale resources at any time from the site's settings.
